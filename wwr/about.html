History

Founded in 2000 in the heart of Chiapas, Mexico, Wild Waves began with a single raft and a big dream—to bring the thrill of whitewater rafting to the rivers of southern Mexico. What started as a small crew of passionate guides navigating the Grijalva River quickly grew into one of the region's most trusted names in outdoor adventure.

Today, Wild Waves continues to lead with heart, offering unforgettable river adventures while honoring the rich ecology and culture of Chiapas. Whether you're paddling through roaring rapids or floating beneath towering canyon walls, every trip with Wild Waves tells a story worth sharing.

What we do?

Wild Waves offers some of Chiapa's most thrilling whitewater rafting. The whitewater professionals at Wild Waves will help all rafters—from experienced paddlers to first-time beginners—find a stretch of river suitable for your desired adventure level. With trips ranging from Class II to Class V, Wild Waves will help make a Chiapas whitewater rafting trip the adventure highlight of your vacation! Whitewater rafting getaways and family rafting adventures in Chiapas are our specialty. Wild Waves operates in a lush, biodiverse valley where stunning Chiapanecan scenery complements your exhilarating whitewater experience. On our more challenging trips, the rapids come one right after another, but if you look carefully, you may spot otters, tapirs, monkeys, or even an occasional jaguar or quetzal! We are conveniently located near Tuxtla Gutiérrez and San Cristóbal de las Casas. Wild Waves also offers daily guided rafting trips on the wild and scenic Grijalva River. Anyone traveling through the Sumidero Canyon region can sign up for a raft trip through the family-friendly El Ocote Biosphere or tackle the exciting Sumidero Canyon more than 1,000 meters below the rim.
