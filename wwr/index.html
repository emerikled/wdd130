5-DAY EXPEDITION ON THE LACANJÁ RIVER

Called “Green Water Snake” by the Mayans, we will travel 81 km on a raft within the boundaries of the Montes Azules Biosphere Reserve (Lacandon Jungle).

3-DAY EXPEDITION ON THE JATATE RIVER

The Jatate River is a crystal-clear river that originates in the Sierra Madre de Chiapas and is known for its stunning landscapes and biodiversity.

2-DAY EXPEDITION IN THE LA VENTA RIVER CANYON

The route along the La Venta River, known as the “Sacred Canyon,” offers a majestic and impressive landscape.

USUMACINTA RIVER EXPEDITION

The Usumacinta River is the largest river in Mexico, receiving water from the Chixoy and La Pasión rivers in Guatemala, and the Ixcan, Chajul, and Lacantum rivers in Mexico. The Usumacinta River flows into the Gulf of Mexico.
